I’ve pretty much abandoned this. Reasons why I stopped:

  • I wasn’t remembering as much as I thought I would. I expected to have a decent knowledge about the cards I’ve written over time but it seemed like I’d only remember the most recent ones. Even when repeatedly using a card or linking to it or thinking about it, I’d lose it after I learned a few more cards.
  • This is unwieldy. So many index cards aren’t fun to flip through and frustrating to handle.
  • Roam is way better. Anki is awesome and I feel like there’s a lot of ways to improve my Anki usage.
